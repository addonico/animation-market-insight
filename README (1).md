# Animation Market Insight (AMI)

A visualization project analyzing the presence and box-office performance of animated films in Mainland China, North America, Japan, and France from 2016 to 2025, and the production companies behind them.

Created for the Information Visualization course at the University of Bologna.

## Research Questions
1. What share of films released in Mainland China, North America (United States/Canada), Japan and France were animated from 2016 to 2025?
2. How does the worldwide box-office performance of top animated films compare with top non-animated films from 2016 to 2025?
3. Which production companies are behind the top animated films, and do companies such as Disney, Pixar and Marvel still dominate the market?
